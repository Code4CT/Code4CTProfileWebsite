Biography of Moletsabisa Molapo

Moletsabisa is one of the most inspirational young woman in the world that use Technology to improve the world. Tsabi is known for her hard work and inspiration to young woman to chase their dreams and change the world to a better place. Moletsabisa Molapo was born and raised in The Kingdom of the Lesotho in South Africa. She is compliting her Master degree in Computer Science at the University of Cape Town(UCT). At UCT she is based in the ICT development Research Centre(ICT40). Her academic interest include the use of Information Communication Technology(ICT) in promoting health and education, User Experience Design and Web Science/ Internet Social Sciences.
